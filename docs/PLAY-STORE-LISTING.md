## Full Description (4000 characters max)

```
Do you know what your car actually costs you per mile?

AutoMate is a clean, fast car expense tracker that answers that question. Log fill-ups, maintenance, and expenses in seconds — then see exactly where your money goes with clear charts and real insights.

No ads. No accounts. No cloud required. Your data stays on your device, always.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

WHY AUTOMATE?

Most car expense apps are bloated with ads, force you to create accounts, or have interfaces that look like they were designed in 2012. AutoMate is different:

• 15-second fill-ups — Smart defaults pre-fill your last station, price, and estimated odometer. Just update what changed and save.

• Real insights, not just numbers — See fuel efficiency trends over time, cost-per-mile breakdowns, and spending by category with interactive charts.

• Genuinely private — No analytics, no tracking, no telemetry. Zero data leaves your device. We don't even have a server.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

FEATURES

◆ Fuel Tracking
Log every fill-up with volume, price, discounts, and location. Handles partial fills correctly in efficiency calculations. Supports gas, diesel, and electric vehicles (kWh).

◆ Service & Maintenance
Record oil changes, tire rotations, brake jobs, and more. Pick from 23 pre-loaded service types or create your own. Never lose track of what was done and when.

◆ Expense Logging
Track registration, insurance, parking, tolls, car washes, and any other vehicle cost. See your true total cost of ownership.

◆ Smart Reminders
Set maintenance reminders by distance, time, or both. Get notified when an oil change or tire rotation is coming due. Visual progress bars show how close you are.

◆ Dashboard
At-a-glance metrics: total spent, cost per mile, and fuel efficiency trend. Filter by period (1 month to all time). Donut chart breaks down spending by fuel, service, and expenses.

◆ Multi-Vehicle Support
Track unlimited vehicles. Switch between them instantly. Each vehicle gets its own fuel type, units, and complete history.

◆ Data Export
Export your complete history as CSV anytime — filter by vehicle and date range. Your data is yours.

◆ Backup & Restore
Back up your entire database to a file. Restore on the same or a different device. No account needed.

◆ VIN Decode
Enter your Vehicle Identification Number to auto-fill year, make, and model. Uses the free NHTSA database.

◆ Works Offline
Everything works without an internet connection. There is no cloud dependency.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

BUILT FOR YOU, NOT FOR ADVERTISERS

AutoMate is built by a solo developer who was tired of car expense apps with intrusive ads, forced logins, and broken cloud sync. This app exists because the alternatives kept getting worse — not better.

• No ads, ever
• No account creation, ever
• No data collection, ever
• No subscription required

Your car data is personal. It should stay that way.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PERMISSIONS (only when you ask)

• Camera — Only when you take a receipt or vehicle photo
• Location — Only when you tap "Use current location" for a station
• Notifications — Only for reminders you create

Download AutoMate and finally know what your car really costs.
```

## Icon Notes

### App Icon (512x512 PNG, 32-bit, no transparency for Play Store)
The icon should be simple and recognizable at small sizes. Consider a stylized car silhouette or speedometer/gauge combined with a dollar sign or chart element. Use the app's primary blue (#3B82F6) as the dominant color on a light background (#F5F4F1). Avoid text in the icon — it becomes unreadable at small sizes.

### Adaptive Icon (Android)
- **Foreground layer:** 108x108 dp (432x432 px at xxxhdpi). The icon artwork within the inner 72x72 dp safe zone.
- **Background layer:** Solid color (#F5F4F1) or a subtle gradient.
- Both layers should be provided as separate PNG files at `./assets/adaptive-icon.png` (foreground) with the background color set in `app.json`.

### Notification Icon (Android)
- **Size:** 96x96 px, white silhouette on transparent background.
- **Format:** PNG with alpha channel.
- **Design:** A simplified version of the app icon — just the core shape, single color (white), no background. Android renders this as a monochrome silhouette in the notification shade.
- **Path:** `./assets/notification-icon.png`

### Play Store Hi-Res Icon
- **Size:** 512x512 px, PNG, 32-bit color, no alpha.
- Same design as the app icon but rendered at high resolution.
