# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Casual car owners who want a clear picture of what their car costs them. Not fleet managers or obsessives — someone who fills up, gets an oil change, and wants those moments captured without friction. They open the app briefly, often right after an event (just fueled up, just left the shop), then close it. The interface should feel like it remembers what they usually do and gets out of the way.

## Product Purpose

AutoMate tracks car expenses — fuel fill-ups, service, and other costs — and turns them into a readable financial and maintenance history. Success looks like: a user who can answer "what did this car cost me last year?" and "when is my next oil change?" without digging through receipts.

## Brand Personality

Sharp, clean, efficient, modern — but never clinical or cold. The app should feel like a well-designed physical notebook that also happens to be smart. Confident and capable without being intimidating.

## References

- **Craft Docs** — context-aware tools that change with the task, premium physical-notebook feel, modular UX that reduces cognitive load
- **Headspace** — warm palette used purposefully, micro-interactions that guide without overwhelming, emotional design that lowers friction
- **Airbnb** — progressive disclosure (one question at a time), frictionless flows, transparent and user-first
- **Todoist** — functional minimalism, intelligent quick-add defaults, feels like a personal assistant not a database, subtle color coding without clutter
- **Play** — spatial design, intelligent defaults, complexity handled invisibly

## Anti-references

Nothing explicit. Avoid anything that feels like a generic utility or finance tracker — the category-reflex of navy + gold, grey dashboards, or spreadsheet-derived UI.

## Design Principles

1. **The app does the thinking.** Intelligent defaults, inferred values, pre-filled fields. The user confirms, not enters.
2. **Progressive disclosure.** Show what's needed for the current task. Don't front-load. One question at a time.
3. **Glanceable over readable.** The dashboard communicates in seconds. Numbers, not paragraphs. Hierarchy that guides the eye.
4. **Warm precision.** Sharp layouts and clean type balanced by subtle warmth — color, radius, motion. Never sterile.
5. **Context-aware surfaces.** The right tools appear for the right task. Forms adapt. Empty states explain. Errors guide.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA. No specific known needs. Standard support for system font scaling and dark mode.
